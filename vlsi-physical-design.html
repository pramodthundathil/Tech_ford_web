VLSI Physical Design

Master industry-grade VLSI physical design skills with hands-on training and live projects.

About Programme

The VLSI Physical Design course at Techford is crafted for aspiring chip design engineers, covering the complete flow from RTL to GDSII using industry-standard EDA tools. This program ensures you gain practical, job-ready skills through real-time projects, guided by experienced trainers with semiconductor industry backgrounds. Topics include floorplanning, power planning, placement, clock tree synthesis, routing, and physical verification. With assignments, case studies, and mock interviews, you’ll be fully prepared for core VLSI roles.

  • Duration: 4–6 Months
  • Mode: Online + Offline
  • Eligibility: B.Tech/BE/M.Tech in ECE, EEE, E&I, or related fields
  • Trainer: 10+ years industry experience

Career Scope

Career Opportunities After VLSI Physical Design

Open the door to high-demand roles in the semiconductor industry with global career opportunities.

Physical Design Engineer

Work on chip floorplanning, placement, routing, and design verification for cutting-edge processors and SoCs.

STA Engineer

Specialize in static timing analysis ensuring high-speed and power-efficient chip performance.

Verification Engineer

Ensure functional correctness of designs through advanced simulation and verification methodologies.

Low-Power Design Engineer

Focus on creating energy-efficient designs for IoT, mobile, and embedded systems.

EDA Tool Specialist

Support semiconductor companies in optimizing design tools and flows for better efficiency.
